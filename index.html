Py Contributors Developers Community

An open Source community primarily for Python.

About the Community

We are a community of developers who are interested in contributing to Python, Javascript, and other open-source projects. Many of us are core developers, but we are not limited to that. we are also interested in contributing to other projects. Most of us are active on Discord and we have a channel for Py-Contributors. We also have a GitHub organization, and we have a few repositories there.

Why you should join the py-contributors community ?

Open source projects bring many benefits to those who participate in them, and such experience is excellent for your CV and more remarkable for your knowledge and skills. By joining a community of like-minded people and polishing up your skills, you can give yourself a step up as an aspiring developer. We are an active developer community encouraging open-source development.
